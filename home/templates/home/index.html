Frequently Asked Questions

What is Blockbuster Streaming App?
Blockbuster Streaming App is a streaming service that offers a wide variety of award-winning TV shows, movies, anime, documentaries and more – on thousands of internet-connected devices.

You can watch as much as you want, whenever you want, without a single ad – all for one low monthly price. There's always something new to discover, and new TV shows and movies are added every week!
Where can I watch?
Watch anywhere, anytime, on an unlimited number of devices. Sign in with your Blockbuster Streaming App account to watch instantly on the web at Blockbuster Streaming App.com from your personal computer or on any internet-connected device that offers the Blockbuster Streaming App app, including smart TVs, smartphones, tablets, streaming media players and game consoles.
How do I cancel?
Blockbuster Streaming App is flexible. There are no annoying contracts and no commitments. You can easily cancel your account online in two clicks. There are no cancellation fees – start or stop your account anytime.
What can I watch on Blockbuster Streaming App?
Blockbuster Streaming App has an extensive library of feature films, documentaries, TV shows, anime, award-winning Blockbuster Streaming App originals, and more. Watch as much as you want, anytime you want.
Is Blockbuster Streaming App good for kids?
The Blockbuster Streaming App Kids experience is included in your membership to give parents control while kids enjoy family-friendly TV shows and films in their own space.

Kids profiles come with PIN-protected parental controls that let you restrict the maturity rating of content kids can watch and block specific titles you don’t want kids to see.
